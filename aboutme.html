About me

Hello! My name is Christian Burgos and I am a 3D modeler who loves to create NPR (Non Photo Realistic) models. I generally create charcter models but plan to also extend to environments and more in the near future.

My start to my 3D modelling journey began in the very beginning of 2023, where I began my first year into game design in Illinois State University. I have always loved games ever since childhood and it has been a thing that has shaped my life and my relationships. I have always been in love with the power of immersion and how one could escape into anew world with such ease and fulfill your dreams and fantasies. I gained this love through my experience with video games and anime and they impacted me in a strong way. My hope is that I can provide that to people and affect others the same way I was affected.
